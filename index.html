B2B SaaS 2025

Manelens

Manelens is a dual-sided hair intelligence platform for consumers and salons. I designed both products end-to-end, across two different user groups with different goals, workflows, and levels of technical literacy.

What I Designed

  • Consumer app onboarding, product and routine recommendations, and the Mane ID consent and sharing flow
  • Salon SaaS dashboard with client CRM, appointment history, inventory analysis, training, certification, and an insight feed
  • A consent system that lets consumers control exactly what professionals can see and act on
Challenge + outcome
Challenge

The consent model had to feel empowering rather than extractive, while still supporting a shared-data experience across consumer and professional products.

Outcome

A scalable two-sided system designed to grow with both consumer adoption and professional network effects.

Fintech AI 2025

Orasis AI

Designed the UI/UX for an AI-powered investment idea generation platform for hedge funds and asset managers. The product surfaces and ranks opportunities daily while producing due diligence documentation for each idea.

What I Designed

  • An Alpha Ideas dashboard with a ranked feed of ten daily opportunities and thumbs up/down feedback that feeds the model
  • A due diligence interface covering thesis, data sources, risk, transparency metrics, and compliance validation
  • Interface patterns that make AI output understandable without flattening the complexity of the underlying analysis
Challenge + outcome
Challenge

Institutional users need to trust how ideas are generated, not just what the recommendations are, so the design had to be transparent and auditable.

Outcome

Supports AI-driven research workflows without losing the analytical rigor required for internal and external due diligence.

Healthcare Marketplace 2025

Carebrij

Carebrij connects patients with medical professionals across specialties. I designed the booking experience, provider profile system, and service discovery flow from the moment someone searches to the moment their appointment is confirmed.

What I Designed

  • Provider profiles with credential display, specialty-specific services, availability, and patient reviews
  • A booking flow with appointment type selection, intake questions, and confirmation states with clear next steps
  • Service discovery built around symptoms and conditions instead of specialty codes
Challenge + outcome
Challenge

Healthcare marketplaces fail when patients cannot confidently evaluate providers they have never heard of, so trust had to be visible throughout the flow.

Outcome

Reduced the decision friction between discovery and booking by making the experience feel closer to a trusted recommendation.

Education Live Product 2024

NACOS Payment

Redesigned the dues payment system for NACOS FUNAAB, now live and processing payments for thousands of students. The redesign focused on eliminating confusion in the payment flow and fixing error states that caused retries.

What I Designed

  • A simplified multi-step payment flow with clear progress indicators and inline validation
  • Error handling with specific, actionable messages instead of generic failures
  • Confirmation and receipt states that give students confidence the transaction went through
Challenge + outcome
Challenge

The existing system was creating double payments and abandonments because students could not tell if a transaction had succeeded.

Outcome

Directly reduced confusion-related support queries around payment completion.

Enterprise Dashboard 2024

Roosta

A role-based attendance tracking and records management system for enterprise teams. I designed the product across two primary roles: managers who need to act quickly, and employees who need to understand their records without escalating to HR.

What I Designed

  • A manager dashboard with attendance overviews, exception flagging, approvals, and reporting views
  • An employee view for personal attendance, leave balances, and self-service request submission
  • A shared component system that keeps role-based interfaces consistent as the product scales
Challenge + outcome
Challenge

Enterprise HR tools tend to accumulate features until the interface becomes the problem, so the design had to surface only what each role needed.

Outcome

A scalable system where each user type can operate independently without requiring training or tribal knowledge.
